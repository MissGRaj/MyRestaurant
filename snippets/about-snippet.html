Flavors of the world on your plate

With a passion to serve the best of world flavors to Kerala folks, King Athul's was started in 2021 in a little nook as a restaurant. Now evolved into Indian European fusion and soon shooting towards a World fusion destination restaurant in cities.

The taste of a freshly made meal is not very easy to find these days and we at King Athul's promise to make everything after you are seated in the restaurant. We source fresh produce to assure the highest quality food. Modern casual ambience combined with attentive staff is sure to make you celebration memorable.
